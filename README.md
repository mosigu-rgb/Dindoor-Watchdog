##  Abstract: Architectures of Power
The shift from conventional military deterrence to AI-driven warfare is accelerating. This research explores the "Gigawatt Era" of compute, the fragility of the global semiconductor supply chain (TSMC/Nvidia), and why the first nation to achieve ASI will hold an irreversible strategic advantage.
